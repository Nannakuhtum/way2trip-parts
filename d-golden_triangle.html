GOLDEN TRIANGLE 04 Nights 05 Days

AGRA, JAIPUR, DELHI

Day 1

Delhi - Agra Pick up Rly Stn. / Airport & drive to Agra. On arrival at the hotel. Proceed for sightseeing tour of the famous Taj Mahal built by Mughal Emperor Shahjehan in 1630 for his Queen Mumtaz Mahal to enshrine her mortal remains. Later visit the Agra Fort containing the Pearl Mosque, the Halls of Public and Private Audience. Also visit the Tomb of Itmad-ud-Daullah built by Empress Noorjehan in memory of his father. Return to Hotel. Overnight at hotel


Day 2

Agra - Fatehpur Sikri - Jaipur After breakfast and check-out and departure to Jaipur visiting Fatehpur Sikri. On arrival at the hotel. Afternoon tour of Jaipur. Jaipur - the capital of Rajasthan was given a color coat of pink a century ago in honor of a visiting Prince and ever since, it has retained this color. Built by Maharaja Jai Singh, the notable astronomer, this city is 260 years old. Visit the Maharaja Palace City, the Observatory & Ram Niwas Gardens. Drive past the Hawa Mahal and through the rose residential areas and business. Overnight in Jaipur


Day 3

Jaipur After breakfast, depart for half day excursion to Amber Fort (Elephant ride at additional cost subject to availability), the ancient capital of the state until 1728. Jagmandir or visit the Hall of Victory glittering with mirrors, Jai Mahal and Temple of Kali. Mount Elephant, the hill on which the fort is situated. Half day afternoon tour the city visiting Maharaja City Palace and the observatory Hawa Mahal-disk or the palace of the winds and the residential sector rose-pink and Albert Museum & Hall Birla Temple. Overnight at Hotel


Day 4

Jaipur - Delhi Departure from the hotel and back to Delhi. Arrival at the hotel. Visit Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb and Lotus Temple. Overnight at hotel


Day 5

Delhi Departure from the hotel and back to Delhi. Arrival at the hotel. Visit Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb and Lotus Temple. Overnight at hotel.
