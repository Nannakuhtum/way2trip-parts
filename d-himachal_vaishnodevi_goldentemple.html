HIMACHAL, VAISHNO DEVI & GOLDEN TEMPLE - 12 Nights 13 Days Package

Shimla(02 Nights)- Manali(03 Nights)- Dharamshala(01 Night)- Dalhousie(02 Nights)- Katra -Vaishno Devi(02 Nights)- Amritsar(01 Night)- Chandigarh(01 Night)

Day 1

Arrival Delhi -Shimla (380 Kms, 9 Hours) Meet and assist. Drive to Shimla-Summer capital of India during British period. On arrival, Shimla Check-in to your Hotel. Rest of the free for Individual activities. Overnight Shimla.


Day 2

Shimla: Full day sightseeing Today enjoy full day sightseeing tour of Shimla Hills visit Chail Valley, Kufri Valley, Fagu Valley. Overnight Shimla


Day 3

Shimla- Sightseeing - Manali (280 Kms, 8 Hours) Today enjoy half day sightseeing tour of Shimla visit- The Ridge, Lakkar Bazar, Jakhoo Hill, St. Michael's Cathedral. Afternoon transfer to Manali.On arrival, Manali check-in to your Hotel. Overnight Manali


Day 4

Manali- Excursion to Rohtang Pass (Snow Point) Today enjoy excursion to Rohtang Pass also called Snow point- enjoy the beauty of year round snow caped mountains. Overnight Manali


Day 5

Manali At leisure Day at leisure on your own activities. Overnight Manali.


Day 6

Manali- Dharmshala ( 230 kms) Travel by surface from Kullu to Mc Leodganj (Dharamsala). On arrival check in at hotel. Evening half day city tour visiting Dal Lake, Church, Bhagsu Nag Temple and Tibetan Monastery. Overnight at hotel


Day 7

Dharmshala - Dalhousie (130 kms ) Morning leave for Dalhousie. Arrive and check in at Hotel. Day at leisure to pamper yourself at the small town famous for its scenic beauty. Overnight stay at Hotel.


Day 8

Dalhousie -Khajjiar - Dalhousie Proceed for sightseeing. Flow through a mesmerizing experience. The vast emerald green meadow of Khajjiar with its thick forested slopes of deodhars, the stupendous mountain views from Lakkar Mandi, the unparalleled beauty of Kalatope and Bakrota Round Overnight at Khajjiar/Dalhousie.


Day 9

Dalhousie - Katra (Vaishno Devi )( 240 Kms 8 Hrs). Early morning check out from hotel and depart for Vaishno Devi (Katra). On arrival check in Hotel. Over Night stay


Day 10

Vaishno Devi (28 Kms Round Trip). Early morning depart for Mata Vaishno Devi Ji Darshan ( Return 28 Kms on your own). Evening return back to hotel. Overnight stay at hotel.


Day 11

Katra - Amritsar (280 Kms, 9 Hours) Early morning check out from hotel and transfer by surface to Amritsar. On arrival check in at hotel. Evening visit the Golden Temple (The famous Sikh pilgrim place), Wagah Border and Jaliawalan Bagh. Overnight at hotel


Day 12

Amritsar - Chandigarh (230 Kms ) After Breakfast drive to Chandigarh. Evening free for your to visit local Chandigarh market. Overnight stay at hotel


Day 13

Chandigarh - Delhi ( 260 kms) Morning city tour of Chandigarh visiting the famous Rock Garden - the lifetime dream of Mr. Nek Chand, Sukhna Lake - the beautiful lake that is the major attraction of Chandigarh. Intime drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival drop at Rly. Stn./Airport for onward journey
