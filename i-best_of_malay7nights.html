Best of Malaysia 8Days/7Nights

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive & Tour Kuala Lumpur - In the heart of Asia lies a land of many cultures, wonders and attractions. Experience Asia in Malaysia. Arrive at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport. After clearing Customs & Immigrations you will be met by our local representative at the Arrival Hall and transferred to your hotel on seat in coach basis. Arrive & Check In at the Hotel. Afternoon proceed on a Half-day Panoramic City tour of Kuala Lumpur on seat in coach basis. Kuala Lumpur offers a variety of historic sites and monuments, including the Jamek Mosque, the oldest Mosque located at the confluence of two rivers, King's Palace, National Museum, and you will also pass through the Parliament House, Supreme Court with its unique moorish architecture (Sultan Abdul Samad building), the Independent Square (Dataran Merdeka), and the busy Chinatown.. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.


Day 2

Fullday Tour of Gentng Highlands (Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. At 1100hrs you will be met by our representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to the famous Malaysian hill-station Genting Highlands. Enroute, visit and tour the fascinating Batu Caves, a series of sacred caverns for the Hindus of Malaysia. Its main attraction is the large statue of the Hindu God at the entrance, besides a steep 272 climb up its steps to view the stunning skyline of the city centre and the magnificent cave temple. The limestone forming Batu Caves is said to be around 400 million years old. You will take the cable car to reach the top of the hill. Then enjoy rides at the Indoor & Outdoor Theme Park s of Genting. The heart-stopping roller coaster and spectacular rides make both young and old screech with delight. You may also try the optional Snow World. Build a snowman, start a snowball fight or just relish the cold nipping at your nose. You can also enjoy the glitter & excitement of the casino and try your luck with the famous international games. After an exciting day, we proceed back to Kuala Lumpur. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.


Day 3

Depart for Penang - After breakfast at hotel you will be picked by our representative to be transferred to the coach station. Here you will board the coach to Penang. Penang - a world renowned exotic holiday destination - was known, once upon a time, as Pulau Pinang or the "Isle of the Betel Nut". Listed as a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage Site in 2008, this virgin paradise has no shortage of cultural sights and natural scenery. Nicknamed the Pearl of the Orient, Penang is famous for its soft sandy beaches and is fondly regarded as the food capital of Malaysia. Some of the most interesting sites of Penang include the sandy beaches of Tanjung Bungah, the landscape from the summit of Penang Hill and the vipers in the Snake Temple. Enroute you will travel across the Famous Penang Bridge. Upon arrival you will be picked up and transferred to your hotel on seat in coach basis. Arrive & Check In at the Hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel in Penang.


Day 4

Tour Penang (Breakfast) - After breakfast at hotel you will be picked up from your hotel lobby in the morning for half day Penang Island tour on seat in coach basis. The Penang Round Island Tour takes you on a journey to some of the island s key attractions; start out the day with a visit to the colourful Tropical Spice Garden. Later visit a traditional fishing village before you head on over to the Butterfly Farm; later stop over at the Snake Temple, where a host of slithery creatures are on display. The tour stops every once in while and you ll get the chance to sample local cuisine at the roadside hawker stalls be sure to try the asam laksa and char kway teow when you get the chance. The day wraps up with a visit to a pewter factory watch how this alloy is made into popular souvenirs. Finally visit the Batik Factory where you can see how this exquisite Malay textile is produced. Rest of the day is free at leisure. Overnight at the hotel, Penang.


Day 5

Cruise on to Langkawi Island (Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel proceed to the jetty to board your cruise to Langkawi. Upon arrival you will be picked up and transferred to your hotel on seat in coach basis. Arrive & Check In at the Hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel in Langkawi.


Day 6

Tour Langkawi (Breakfast) - Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Today you will be picked up from your hotel lobby in the morning for half day Langkawi Island tour on seat in coach basis. Langkawi is an island of tranquil and tropical beauty that awaits to be discovered. Your tour will begin with a visit to the hot springs believed to have medicinal values followed by a visit to the Black Sand Beach to see the unique color due to heavy deposits of mineral oxides. Later visit the Mahsuri Tomb built to remember the legend of the Princess Mahsuri who was wrongly accused of adultery. Visit the Marble factory prior to the final stop at the Duty free shopping Centre at Kuah Town. Return to the hotel. Rest of the day is free at leisure. Overnight at the hotel, Langkawi.


Day 7

Depart for Kuala Lumpur Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Check Out of the hotel by noon to be transferred to the airport. Upon arrival in KL you will be met by our local representative to transfer you to the hotel. Afternoon is free for leisure activities. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur. Free passes to KL Tower will be provided. *You can opt to take the Optional Night Tour of Kuala Lumpur Evening you will be picked up to tour Kuala Lumpur at night. Inspired by Malaysia s rich and exotic heritage, the Cultural Night Tour not only showcases the country s abundant allure and beauty, but also showcases its ancient traditions mingled with cosmopolitan appeal. The tour kicks off with a visit to the vivacious Chinatown; known to locals as Petaling Street, Next up, is the Sri Mahamariamman Temple; dating from 1873, this historic temple is a large and ornate house of worship for the Hindu community of KL. You will also get to know why Kuala Lumpur is known as the City of Lights during the course of this tour. Visit the KL Tower. This telecommunications and broadcasting tower actually appears to be taller than the Petronas Towers, because it is built on a hill. Here you can experience the lives of the metropolitan city, breathtaking sunset and amazing night views at the height of 276 meters above ground level. You can do so at the comfort of the Observation Deck. There after proceed for an Indian Dinner.


Day 8

Depart Kuala Lumpur (Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Check Out of the hotel by noon. You will be met by our local representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to KL International Airport for your flight back home.
