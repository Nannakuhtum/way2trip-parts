Dubai 3 Nights/4Days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive in Dubai Welcome to Dubai. You will be transferred from the airport to your hotel (transfers on seat in coach basis). The rest of the day is yours to relax, explore the city or go shopping. Dubai is an Arab city with a highly international and cosmopolitan look and feel. Overnight at your hotel.


Day 2

Desert Safari tour with BBQ Dinner After breakfast, the morning is free for you to relax. In the afternoon proceed on your Desert Safari tour. While travelling across the deserts of Dubai, you will have several photo stops during an exciting dune drive to the first destination, the camel farm. The drive continues across the desert where you will have the opportunity to witness a beautiful desert sunset, one of the highlights of a trip to the Arabian deserts. Upon arrival at your campsite, you have the opportunity to ride on a camel, experience the thrill of sand boarding and try out a henna design on hands or feet. After all that activity you will have worked up an appetite, so enjoy a delicious BBQ dinner and a shisha (the famous Arabic water pipe). Enjoy a belly dancer performing her show around the campfire by starlight, before returning to your hotel. Overnight at your hotel in Dubai. (B, D)


Day 3

Sightseeing Today after breakfast, you decide what you would like to do. After breakfast, you may want to explore the city on your own, drop in at one of the many themed malls to shop and be entertained (a few have indoor amusement parks and the Mall of Emirates even has a ski run with real snow); or take in an amusement park like the Wild Wadi water park which has attractions like Master Blasters, Flow Riders, Wave Pool, Ring Ride and a lot more. (Note that all optional tour and activities today are at your own cost and are not included in the price of the tour). In the evening, proceed for Dhow Cruise and transfer back to the hotel. Overnight at your hotel in Dubai. (B)


Day 4

Fly back home After breakfast, say good bye to this amazing city as you are transferred to the airport for your flight back home (transfers on seat in coach basis). The tour ends at Dubai Airport. (B)
