HOLYLAND PILGRIM TOUR - Jordan - Israel - Egypt - 8 Nights 9 days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Chennai - Amman- Mount Nebo Arrival at QueenAlia Airport, AMMAN, meet & assist through all formalities. Travel through The King's Highway, the world's oldest continuously used trading route to visit Mt Nebo.(Memorial of Prophet Moses, the presumed site of his death & burial place. He was given a view of the Promised Land from Mt Nebo. From there, one can view Jordan valley & the Dead Sea). Proceed to hotel.


Day 2

Amman-Nazareth-Cana Breakfast at hotel. Proceed to Sheik Hussain Bridge to cross the border. Complete the customs, emigration formalities and enter into Israel. Lunch at restaurant. Visit Nazareth, the boyhood town of Jesus and the Basilica of the Annunciation, where Archangel Gabriel appeared to announce that Mary would bear a son. Stop by Joseph's workshop and Cana, where Jesus performed his first miracle, of changing water into wine at the wedding feast. Dinner and overnight at hotel.


Day 3

Sea of Galilee-Capernaum-Jordan River-Nazareth - Bethlehem Breakfast at hotel. Visit Mount Tabor, Capernaum (the centre of Jesus' ministry after he left Nazareth) stop at Tabgha - the Church of the Multiplication (of the Five Loaves and Two Fishes) and the Church of the Beatitudes, where Jesus gave the Sermon on the Mount. Drive through Galilee, and ride in a Galilean fishing boat. Visit St. Peter's house, the Church of Primacy of Peter, and Mensa Christi. Lunch at restaurant. Visit Yardenit on the Jordan River, the traditional Baptismal site. Visit Jericho, Mount of Temptation and Sycamore Tree. It is the place where Jesus stopped on the way to Jerusalem on the Eve of the Passover, and where Jesus cured the blind man during his stay at Zachaeus house. Proceed to Bethlehem. Dinner and overnight at hotel.


Day 4

Old City Of Jerusalem-Golgotha-Wailing Wall-Ein Karem Breakfast at hotel. Proceed to the pools of Beit Hasda, where Jesus cured the crippled man. Walk through the Via Dolorosa (Way of Grief), traditionally accepted as the last route trodden by Jesus. Follow the 14 Stations of the Cross to Golgotha, place where Jesus was crucified marking significant events along the route. Visit the Pilate's Judgments Hall, the Chapel of the Flagellation (where the Roman soldiers scourged Jesus and placed a crown of thorns on His head), the Ecce Homo Arch and the Convent of the Sisters of Zion. The last 5 Stations of the Cross are inside the Church of the Holy Sepulcher visible from a distance with two silver domes. Also "The Tomb" (widely believed to be the part of the stone that covered Jesus Tomb). Visit the Cenacle - Last Supper Room, the tomb of King David, the Wailing Wall, which is the holiest place for the Jewish religion. Lunch. Visit Ein Karem, the Birthplace of John the Baptist, and the Church of Visitation. Proceed to Dead Sea, where we stop at a private beach. It is the lowest spot on earth and do not miss the opportunity to float in the salty buoyant water, rich in minerals. Move to Bethania, the Town of Mary, Martha and Lazarus. Visit Lazarus's Tomb. Dinner and overnight at hotel.


Day 5

Mount of Olives-Gethsemane-Bethlehem Breakfast at hotel, Proceed to Mount of Olives for a panoramic view of Jerusalem. Visit the sanctuary of Pater Noster Ascension. Walk along the Palm Sunday road down to the chapel of Dorminous Flevit (where Jesus wept over the destruction of Jerusalem) and Garden of Gethsemani.(where Jesus prayed while Judah betrayed him to the Romans). Visit the Basilica of agony, Tomb of St. Mary, Church of St. Peter of Gallicantu,( which symbolizes Peter's denial of Jesus as he has prophesied at the Last Supper) and visit the traditional house of High Priest, Caiaphas, where Jesus was imprisoned. Move to Bethlehem to visit the Shepherd's field, in the Judah province. Lunch. Visit the Church of Nativity, the birthplace of Jesus and the City of David, Manager Square, Crusaders Cloister, Grotto of St. Jerome and the Milk Grotto. Dinner and overnight at hotel.


Day 6

Taba - Sinai Breakfast at hotel. Check-out hotel. Proceed to Taba. Lunch at Restaurant. . Enter into Egypt after formalities proceed to Sinai. View the beautiful and historical Sinai Mountains. Check-in hotel. Dinner and overnight at hotel.


Day 7

Sinai - St.Catherine Monastry Breakfast at hotel. Visit St. Catherine Monastery set beneath the mountain where Moses is said to have received the Ten Commandments. It has been one of the world's great centers of religious pilgrimage for over fifteen centuries. It has a library of ancient manuscripts, second only to the Vatican and a 6th century church on the site of the Burning Bush. Lunch at restaurant. On the way to Cairo, we cross the famous Suez Canal. Evening, a cruise on the River Nile. Dinner on board. Enjoy the shows on board. Transfer and overnight at hotel.


Day 8

Cairo- Pyramid,Sphinx - Muscat Breakfast at hotel and checkout. Visit the Pyramids, Sphinx, old churches of Cairo and Egyptian Museum. Lunch. Proceed to; Airport. Departure from Cairo


Day 9

Muscat - Chennai Departs from Muscat. Arrive Chennai
