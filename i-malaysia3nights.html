Malaysia 3 Nights/4Days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive Kuala Lumpur - In the heart of Asia lies a land of many cultures, wonders and attractions. Experience Asia in Malaysia. Arrive at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport. After clearing Customs & Immigrations you will be met by our local representative at the Arrival Hall and transferred to your hotel on seat in coach basis. Arrive & Check In at the Hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.


Day 2

Tour Kuala Lumpur (Breakfast) - Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Today in the morning enjoy a Half-day Panoramic City tour of Kuala Lumpur on seat in coach basis. Kuala Lumpur offers a variety of historic sites and monuments, including the Jamek Mosque, the oldest Mosque located at the confluence of two rivers, King's Palace, National Museum, and you will also pass through the Parliament House, Supreme Court with its unique moorish architecture (Sultan Abdul Samad building), the Independent Square (Dataran Merdeka), and the busy Chinatown. Afternoon is free for leisure activities. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur. Free passes to Kl Tower will be provided. *You can opt to take the Optional Night Tour of Kuala Lumpur Evening you will be picked up to tour Kuala Lumpur at night. Inspired by Malaysia s rich and exotic heritage, the Cultural Night Tour not only showcases the country s abundant allure and beauty, but also showcases its ancient traditions mingled with cosmopolitan appeal. The tour kicks off with a visit to the vivacious Chinatown; known to locals as Petaling Street, Next up, is the Sri Mahamariamman Temple; dating from 1873, this historic temple is a large and ornate house of worship for the Hindu community of KL. You will also get to know why Kuala Lumpur is known as the City of Lights during the course of this tour. Visit the KL Tower. This telecommunications and broadcasting tower actually appears to be taller than the Petronas Towers, because it is built on a hill. Here you can experience the lives of the metropolitan city, breathtaking sunset and amazing night views at the height of 276 meters above ground level. You can do so at the comfort of the Observation Deck. There after proceed for an Indian Dinner.


Day 3

Fullday Tour of Gentng Highlands (Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. At 1100hrs you will be met by our representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to the famous Malaysian hill-station Genting Highlands. Enroute, visit and tour the fascinating Batu Caves, a series of sacred caverns for the Hindus of Malaysia. Its main attraction is the large statue of the Hindu God at the entrance, besides a steep 272 climb up its steps to view the stunning skyline of the city centre and the magnificent cave temple. The limestone forming Batu Caves is said to be around 400 million years old. You will take the cable car to reach the top of the hill. Then enjoy rides at the Indoor & Outdoor Theme Park s of Genting. The heart-stopping roller coaster and spectacular rides make both young and old screech with delight. You may also try the optional Snow World. Build a snowman, start a snowball fight or just relish the cold nipping at your nose. You can also enjoy the glitter & excitement of the casino and try your luck with the famous international games. After an exciting day, we proceed back to Kuala Lumpur. Overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.


Day 4

Day 4 : Depart Kuala Lumpur (Breakfast) - Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Check Out of the hotel by noon. You will be met by our local representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to KL International Airport for your flight back home.
