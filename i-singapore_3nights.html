Best of Singapore 3Nights/4Days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive Singapore (Breakfast) Arrive at the Singapore International Airport. After clearing Customs & Immigration you will be met by our local representative at the Arrival Hall and transferred to your hotel on seat in coach basis. Arrive & Check In at the Hotel. Rest of the day free for your personal activities. Overnight stay at the hotel in Singapore. FREE Indian Dinner voucher will be provided to you. Optional Night Safari Tour: Evening proceed for Night Safari, an entire open-air zoo set in a humid tropical forest, which features the survival and predatory skills of our nocturnal animals. You can see the free flying bats & giant flying squirrels at the Leopard Trail and other performance by tram or on foot at the three walking trails.


Day 2

Tour City of Singapore (Breakfast) Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy a half day city tour of Singapore on seat in coach basis. The City Experience Tour showcases the contrast between old and new and the blend of East and West. Experience the history, culture and the lifestyle of multi-racial Singapore, the exciting heart of New Asia-Singapore. We will drive past the Colonial district for a view of Padang, The Esplanade - Theatres on the Bay and The Merlion a mythical beast, Singapore's original half lion, half fish. Next stop, is a visit to Little India, Chinatown and Gem Factory. Proceed back to the hotel for the overnight stay in Singapore. Optional Sentosa Island Tour: After lunch proceed to Sentosa Island. Visit the Underwater World, Asia's most popular oceanarium and home to more than 2,500 marine animals. Delight yourself at the Dolphin Lagoon and get a rare opportunity to interact with the charming pink dolphins. We wrap up the evening with the 'Songs of the Sea' Show, which is a spectacular display of light and sound show.


Day 3

Free Day to Explore Singapore (Breakfast) Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Today the day is free for leisure activities. Overnight at your hotel in Singapore. Optional Fullday Universal Studio Tour: Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Today enjoy your day at the Universal Studios. Enter the thrilling world of movie magic as you "ride the movies" on roller coasters and other movie-themed attractions. Be dazzled and delighted by an immersive entertainment experience that brings the silver screen to life. Return to the hotel.


Day 4

Depart Singapore (Breakfast) Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Check Out of the hotel by noon. You will be met by our local representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to Singapore International Airport for your flight back home.
