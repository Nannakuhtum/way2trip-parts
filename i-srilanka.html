Srilanka 5Nights/6Days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

(SEEDUWA - DAMBULLA - 150 KMS, 04 HRS Approx. ) Arrive to the airport and transfer to Dambulla / Habarana, Check in at the hotel, Late afternoon visit Dambulla cave temple, Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Dambulla / Habarana.


Day 2

(DAMBULLA - POLONNARUWA - SIGIRIYA - DAMBULLA. ) Breakfast at the Hotel. Proceed to the 02nd Kingdom of Srilanka, Polonnaruwa the ancient city, do a city tour, Afternoon visit the Sigiriya Rock Fortress (These 02 sites can be changed in the morning & evening according to your convenience.) Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Dambulla / Habarana.


Day 3

(DAMBULLA - MATALE - KANDY, 110 KMS, 3 HRS Approx.) Breakfast at the hotel, Proceed to Kandy, enroute do a spice tour at a spice garden, lunch at a restaurant, Check in at the hotel, Evening do a city tour of Kandy, visit the temple of the tooth relic and witness the cultural performance. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel - Kandy.


Day 4

( KANDY - PERADENIYA - RAMBODA - NUWARA ELIYA - 90 KMS, 2 1/2 HRS APPROX ) Breakfast at the hotel and proceed to NuwaraEliya, enroute visit the tea plantation, Check in at the hotel, Overnight stay at the hotel - NuwaraEliya.


Day 5

(NUWARA ELIYA - HATTON - KITULGALA - COLOMBO - 220 KMS. 7 HRS APPROX) Breakfast at the hotel and transfer to Colombo via Kitulgala, Evening do a city tour of Colombo, Overnight stay at the hotel - Colombo


Day 6

(COLOMBO - AIRPORT 40 KMS, 01 HR APPROX) Breakfast at the hotel, and proceed to the airport for departure. End of Tour.

Inclusions:-
  • 05 Nights accommodation as per the itinerary,
  • Transport in a private A/C vehicle with the service of an English speaking chauffeur.
  • 2N accommodation - breakfast in Dambulla at Pelwehera Village Resort or similar
  • 1N accommodation - breakfast in Kandy at Swiss Residence or similar
  • 1N accommodation - breakfast in Nuwara Eliya at Oakray Summer Hill Breeze or similar

Exclusion :-
  • Entrance Fees to any sites.
  • Any additional meal not mentioned on the above program during the tour.
  • Early check in or late checkout charges.
