Thailand 4Nights/5Days

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive Thailand & proceed to Pattaya Meet and greet upon arrival at Bangkok airport by our representative and transferred to Pattaya hotel. Pattaya is nestled along a picturesque bay on the East Coast of the Gulf of Thailand. A fascinating escape where tourists, holiday makers and vacationers from around the world unfold an incomparable array of possibilities to unwind during an exotic holiday beach vacation. Rest of the day is free at leisure for individual sightseeing or shopping. Proceed for half day Four Regions Floating Market tour in Pattaya. Four Regions Floating Market or Pattaya Floating Market opened to the public in November 2008 claimed to be to be the biggest floating market in the eastern region featuring 111 boutique-shops selling native food and specialties from all over Thailand. The market is divided into 4 separate part, each represents each part of Thailand (North, Central, North-Eastern, and South) offering a special blend of differing traditions. The market covering an area of 100,000 square meters (67 rai) was built at a cost of 350 million baht by local young tycoon Warida Sae-eung. Overnight stay at the hotel in Pattaya.


Day 2

Tour Pattaya Coral Island (Breakfast, Lunch) After breakfast proceed for a full day Koh Larn (Coral Island) Tour. Koh Larn (Coral Island) is the largest of Pattaya's archipelago. Major attractions include viewing coral reefs from glass-bottomed boats, snorkelling, scuba diving, windsurfing, skiing and Para-sailing. Lunch included in the tour. Overnight stay at the hotel in Pattaya.


Day 3

Transferred to Bangkok (Breakfast) After breakfast transfer to Bangkok. Bangkok is one of Asia s most cosmopolitan cities. Created as the Thai capital in 1782 by the first monarch of the present Chakri dynasty, Bangkok is a national treasure house and Thailand s spiritual, cultural, political, commercial, educational and diplomatic centre. Major tourist attractions include glittering Buddhist temples, palaces, timeless Venice of the East canal and river scenes, classical dance extravaganzas, and numerous shopping centers. Overnight stay at hotel in Bangkok.


Day 4

Tour Bangkok (Breakfast) After breakfast proceed for a half day city sightseeing tour of Bangkok. A chance to study every day Thai life as you start the tour by making your way through the bustling streets of Bangkok s Chinatown the heart of the old business area of the city heading towards three of the most impressive Buddhist temples in Bangkok. First stop is at Wat Trimit , the Temple of Golden Buddha, which houses an impressive three meters tall, 5.5 ton, solid gold Buddha sculpted in the graceful Sukhothai style. This is followed by Wat Pho , the Temple of the Reclining Buddha, for which you would require a whole list of superlatives to give justice to any description! It is the oldest and largest temple in Bangkok; it feathers the largest reclining Buddha and the largest collection of Buddha images in Thailand; and it was the earliest centre for public education. Enjoy a 30 minute foot massage at Bangkok. Overnight stay at hotel in Bangkok.


Day 5

Depart Bangkok (Breakfast) Enjoy buffet breakfast at the hotel. Check Out of the hotel by noon. You will be met by our local representative at your hotel lobby for your transfer to Bangkok International Airport for your flight back home.
